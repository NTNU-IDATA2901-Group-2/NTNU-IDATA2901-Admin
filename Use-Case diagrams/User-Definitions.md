

# User Definitions
This is an overview of the different users of the application. Included in the definitions are descriptions of who the users are, their technological knowhow, what they work with and what they want to acheive. 

## Administrator
The developer is an employee at solwr. They work on developing the different software shoultions, weather that is backend or frontend, for solwr. They have a good understanding of thecnology, and know their way around web applications and database solutions. The developer wants to spend as much of their time as possible on development. This mean having a change note documentation system that is as fast as possible to use.

## Customer
The customer is an external user, that uses solwr's software solutions for their daily works. They primarily work in management positions, as these roles are the ones communicating with solwr directly. The technical knowhow of the customer is varying, but one has to assume that they have no technological  knowledge to cover all basis. The goal the customer wants to acheive with the application, is to get information of what changes have been made to the application and what value have have gotten in return for their investments. 

## Visitor
A visitor is a third party user that is not required to have any affiliation to solwr or their customers. This user must be assumed to have no technical knowledge. They can work in any sector, but are most likely to work in logistics as they visit solowrs website. These users may want to get as much information about solwrs software lineup as possible, with the potential of becomming a future customer. They want a quick and easy way to get information.
